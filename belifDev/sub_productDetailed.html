DESCRIPTION

AQUA BOMB SLEEPING MASK

This innovative jelly-pudding sleeping mask replenishes and revives dull, dry skin with explosive hydration for a dewy, radiant glow.

Born from the DNA of belif's bestselling Aqua Bomb moisturizer, this overnight mask rejuvenates and replenishes dull, dry, and fatigued skin with explosive hydration for smoother, more supple-looking skin and a healthy glow. The 'memory' formula of this unique jelly-pudding sleeping mask blankets and wraps around the contours of skin with a refreshing layer of intense hydration. Filled with ultra-hydrating herbs like skin-soothing Scottish heather and lady's mantle, the pillow-proof formula is lightweight and absorbs quickly for fuss-free hydration.

OUR PRINCIPLES

This product is dermatologist-tested and free of mineral oils, petrolatum, synthetic preservatives, dyes, fragrances, and animal origin ingredients.

HOW TO USE

Suggested Usage

After or instead of moisturizer, apply a thin layer evenly across your face. Leave on overnight and rinse off in the morning for ultra-hydrated, nourished skin.

  • 1. Gently massage into skin for extra deep hydration. Use when you want your skin to be smoother and more makeup-ready in the mornings!
  • 2. Use after being exposed to extreme wind, heat, or cold to nourish and hydrate skin
  • 3. Store in refrigerator for extra cooling benefits
